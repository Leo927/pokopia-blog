## Overview

Pokopia has 12 legendary and mythical Pokémon, and none of them show up in normal habitats. Each one requires its own unlock method — from placing dolls and visiting Dream Islands, to constructing massive buildings, to crafting bells and solving a 27-piece puzzle. Legendaries also provide the biggest Environment Level boost of any Pokémon, so parking one in a struggling area is the fastest way to level it up.

This guide covers every legendary, what you need, and the most efficient order to tackle them.

## The Five Unlock Methods

| Method | Description | Pokémon |
|--------|-------------|---------|
| **Story** | Unmissable encounters during main quests | Kyogre, Volcanion |
| **Dream Island** | Place a doll → visit the unlocked island | Raikou, Entei, Suicune, Mewtwo |
| **Building Kit** | Purchase/find kit → gather materials → build | Articuno, Zapdos, Moltres |
| **Bell Summoning** | Complete a trio → craft a bell → summon | Lugia, Ho-Oh |
| **Slate Puzzle** | Collect 27 Mysterious Slates → solve mural | Mew |

## Story Legendaries: Kyogre & Volcanion

These two are freebies — you encounter them during the main story and can't miss them.

### Kyogre — Withered Wasteland

Kyogre appears during the "Yawn Up A Storm" quest. Head to the dock at the far end of Withered Wasteland, near where Magikarp hang out. It's story-scripted, so you can't mess this up. *Note: it's currently unconfirmed whether Kyogre can be moved into a habitat after the encounter.*

### Volcanion — Rocky Ridges

Volcanion drops in during the Rocky Ridges main story quest. Like Kyogre, it's an unmissable encounter. After the story meeting, Volcanion can also appear on a Dream Island unlocked with a Clefairy Doll.

## Dream Island Legendaries

Four legendaries hide on Dream Islands. Place the right doll in your home, visit the island it unlocks, and hope the legendary spawns. It's random — plan for multiple trips.

### Doll-to-Legendary Mapping

| Doll | Legendary | Type | Specialty | Story Prerequisite |
|------|-----------|------|-----------|-------------------|
| Pikachu Doll | **Raikou** | Electric | Generate | Must encounter at Bleak Beach first |
| Arcanine Doll | **Entei** | Fire | Burn | None |
| Eevee Doll | **Suicune** | Water | Water | None |
| Dragonite Doll | **Mewtwo** | Psychic | Teleport | Must complete Sparkling Skylands rebuild |

### Key Details

- **Raikou** requires you to encounter it during the Bleak Beach main story first. The Pokédex registration alone isn't enough to befriend it — you must then revisit via the Pikachu Doll Dream Island.
- **Entei and Suicune** have no story prerequisite. You can encounter them as soon as you have the dolls and Dream Island access.
- **Mewtwo** requires completing the "Rebuild the huge building!" request in Sparkling Skylands — a multi-phase construction project (2F through 4F). After reaching the rooftop for the story encounter, use the Dragonite Doll Dream Island to befriend it.

**Pro tip:** Dolls are found on Dream Islands themselves. Visit frequently and collect every doll you find — they're the keys to legendary encounters.

## Legendary Birds: Articuno, Zapdos, Moltres

The legendary birds require building specific structures in **Palette Town**. You need the area unlocked, 15 Pokémon living there, and significant material stockpiles.

### Articuno — Freezing Chambers

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Stone | 50 |
| Ice | 50 |
| Crystal Fragments | 10 |
| Pokémetal | 10 |

The Freezing Chambers Kit is found on a nearby island around Palette Town. The hardest material here is **Ice** — it's surprisingly rare. Farm it from cold areas and icy Dream Islands well in advance.

### Zapdos — Abandoned Power Plant

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Copper Ingot | 50 |
| Gold Ingot | 50 |
| Sea Glass Fragments | 50 |
| Brick | 50 |
| Pokémetal | 10 |

The Abandoned Power Plant Kit is purchased from the Pokémon Center shop. This is the most material-intensive bird build. Start stockpiling Gold Ingots early — Gold Ore is rarer than Copper and Iron in Rocky Ridges mines.

### Moltres — Altar of the Flame

| Material | Quantity |
|----------|----------|
| Copper Ingot | 50 |
| Gold Ingot | 50 |
| Lava Block | 50 |
| Pokémetal | 10 |

The Altar of the Flame Kit is a pickup on islands near Palette Town. Lava Blocks come from volcanic areas in Rocky Ridges.

**Building order suggestion:** Start with whichever bird uses materials you already have stockpiled. If you've been farming Rocky Ridges, Moltres or Zapdos may be easiest first.

## Bell Summoning: Lugia & Ho-Oh

These are the longest unlock chains in the game. You'll see Lugia and Ho-Oh flying overhead long before you can befriend them. To summon one, you need to complete an entire legendary trio first, then craft a special bell from rare materials.

### Lugia — Tidal Bell

**Prerequisite:** Befriend all three legendary birds (Articuno, Zapdos, Moltres).

1. Completing the bird trio unlocks the **Tidal Bell recipe**
2. Collect **Silver Feathers** — Lugia drops these as it flies overhead. Use Cut to dispel the wind around each feather before picking it up
3. Craft the Tidal Bell: **5 Rare Pokémetal + 5 Silver Feathers**
4. Place the bell in an open area. When Lugia flies overhead, ring the bell to summon it

### Ho-Oh — Clear Bell

**Prerequisite:** Befriend all three legendary beasts (Raikou, Entei, Suicune).

1. Completing the beast trio unlocks the **Clear Bell recipe**
2. Collect **Rainbow Feathers** — Ho-Oh drops these as it flies overhead. The feathers land **on fire** — use Water Gun to extinguish the flames before picking them up
3. Craft the Clear Bell: **5 Rare Pokémetal + 5 Rainbow Feathers**
4. Place the bell at a high, open location. When Ho-Oh flies overhead, ring the bell to summon it

**Important:** Raikou's story encounter at Bleak Beach does NOT count toward the beast trio. You must fully befriend Raikou via the Dream Island. Plan accordingly — this is a common trap.

### Bell Crafting Summary

| Bell | Summons | Prerequisite | Materials |
|------|---------|-------------|-----------|
| Tidal Bell | Lugia | Befriend 3 birds | 5 Rare Pokémetal + 5 Silver Feathers |
| Clear Bell | Ho-Oh | Befriend 3 beasts | 5 Rare Pokémetal + 5 Rainbow Feathers |

## Mew — The Slate Puzzle

Mew is the ultimate collector's challenge. You need to find all **27 types of Mysterious Slates** scattered across all five areas, then place them on a mural wall at Withered Wasteland.

- Some slates are obvious pickups while exploring
- Others require specific moves to reach (Rock Smash hidden walls, Surf to hidden caves)
- Some require specific time-of-day conditions
- Search every corner of every area — slates are well-hidden

Once all 27 are placed on the wall, Mew appears and can be befriended. Mew has the **Teleport** specialty, making it one of the most useful legendaries functionally.

## Recommended Unlock Order

Here's the most efficient path for tackling all legendaries:

1. **Kyogre & Volcanion** — automatic through story, no planning needed
2. **Entei & Suicune** — no story prerequisites, just need dolls and Dream Island access
3. **Raikou** — requires Bleak Beach story completion first, then Pikachu Doll
4. **Legendary Birds** — start stockpiling materials for Palette Town builds mid-game
5. **Ho-Oh** — unlocks after befriending the three beasts (Raikou, Entei, Suicune)
6. **Mewtwo** — requires Sparkling Skylands rebuild completion
7. **Lugia** — unlocks after befriending the three birds (Articuno, Zapdos, Moltres)
8. **Mew** — ongoing slate collection throughout the entire game; finish last

The beast trio (→ Ho-Oh) is generally faster than the bird trio (→ Lugia) because Dream Island encounters don't require material farming — just dolls and patience. Start the bird building projects early so materials are ready when you reach Palette Town.

## Tips for Serious Players

- **Legendary Pokémon boost Environment Levels significantly.** Once you befriend one, place it in your weakest area to fast-track its level.
- **Feather collection is time-gated.** Lugia and Ho-Oh fly overhead on their own schedule. Watch the skies and always be ready with Cut (Silver Feathers) or Water Gun (Rainbow Feathers).
- **Rare Pokémetal is the ultimate bottleneck.** You need 10 total for both bells. Start hoarding Pokémetal Fragments early and smelt every chance you get.
- **Dream Islands rotate.** Visit every day. Even if you don't find a legendary, you'll collect dolls, materials, and other resources that feed into the unlock chains.
